About

A Multitalented, versatile, Award-Winning* Performer & Engineer

From stage productions, to a career as a touring musician and speaker, to working professionally as a post-production engineer for both audio and video, Anthony has taken an exceptionally circuitous route to voice over and narration. Along the way, he’s been able to learn the art of production and communication from every conceivable angle.
